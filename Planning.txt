Instructions: describe what the 4 basic functions should do


Describe "moveRight"
	It "should change a player's x coordinate by adding 1"

Describe "moveLeft"
	It "should change a player's x coordinate by subtracting 1"

Describe "moveUp"
	It "should change a player's y coordinate by subtracting 1"

Describe "moveDown"
	It "should change a player's y coordinate by adding 1"
